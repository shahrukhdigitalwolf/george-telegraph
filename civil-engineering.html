Department Of Civil Engineering

Plans for, construction of, and upkeep of artificial structures like roads, rivers, and dams are all part of civil engineering. Established as the second most important field of engineering after military engineering, the name is meant to make it clear that it is not related to the armory.

Application Form

Eligibility : Passed Class X Board Exam.     Duration : 12 Months

Prospective Areas of Work:

  • Empowered by CAD, on completion of this course student can work as a draughtsman in any civil construction work such as buildings, culverts, bridges and roadwork. Public works departments, Municipal bodies and Engineering organisations have converted to computer aided drafting so the course provides an essential additional qualification to enable employment as a draughtsman-cum-CAD operator.
