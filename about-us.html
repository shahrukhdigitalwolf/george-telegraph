NSDC Affiliation

The National Skill Development Corporation India (NSDC) stands as a unique Public-Private Partnership within the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India. Its primary mission is to catalyze the creation of large, high-quality, for-profit vocational institutions, thereby promoting skill development across various sectors.

Established as part of the National Skill Development Mission, NSDC addresses the increasing demand in India for skilled manpower and endeavors to narrow the gap between the demand and supply of skills. Aligned with the National Policy on Skill Development (NPSD), the government has set an ambitious target of skilling 500 million individuals by 2022.

NSDC's core objective is to skill or upskill 150 million people in India by 2022, with a focus on rural areas. This is to be achieved by fostering private sector initiatives in skill development programs and providing viability gap funding. NSDC's purview encompasses skills from both organized and unorganized sectors, ensuring a comprehensive approach to skill enhancement.

Obtaining NSDC certification comes with several advantages. Firstly, the certification is government-approved and industry-validated, instilling credibility. Certified candidates find their details listed in the Government Directory, enhancing the official recognition of their skills. The certification also creates opportunities for upskilling, increasing employability with higher wage rates. Importantly, NSDC certification serves as a gateway to international opportunities, expanding employability prospects globally. The tamper-proof nature of the certificates adds to their authenticity and reliability, making them valuable assets for individuals seeking to advance their skills and career prospects. In essence, NSDC plays a pivotal role in shaping a skilled and employable workforce, contributing significantly to India's journey towards a knowledge-driven economy. NSDC's collaborative approach involves working closely with various industry partners to design and implement skill development initiatives that align with current market demands.

Our Heritage

Established on May 16, 1920, in response to the pressing issue of rising unemployment, The George Telegraph Training Institute emerged as a registered trust committed to human resource development, aligning itself with the evolving needs of the Indian industry. As a trailblazer in the realm of education in India, the institute's inception marked a crucial period just after the First World War. During this time, India witnessed a shift from an agrarian economy in recession to accelerated industrial growth, accompanied by a surge in urbanization and heightened expectations among the youth for meaningful employment and career opportunities.

Despite the increased levels of education, there existed a stark misalignment between the skills imparted and the job requirements of the rapidly expanding Indian industry. Recognizing this gap, Mr Haripada Dutta, a visionary with keen commercial insight, founded GTTI to address the unmet needs of the workforce.

In its early stages, GTTI identified the burgeoning demand for telegraphy, an effective communication method introduced in the Indian subcontinent. The institute swiftly adapted, offering courses such as Telegraphy Railway Coaching, Guard, and Assistant Station Master's Diploma, catering to the industry's requirements.

Within a short span, George Telegraph gained recognition as a center of excellence, earning accolades from esteemed bodies such as the British Army, the British Railway Board, the Ministry of Communications-Government of India, the Department of Labour-Government of India, Railway Board, New Delhi, Maritime Authorities, and various Chambers of Commerce.

Over the years, the institute has evolved to offer a comprehensive array of training programs in electronics engineering, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, civil engineering, commercial practice, communicative studies, and computer software. With a network of more than 50 centers in eastern India, George Telegraph Training Institute continues to play a pivotal role in shaping the skills and careers of the nation's youth, remaining dedicated to its founding vision of addressing the dynamic needs of the industry.

Board of Studies

The George Telegraph Training maintains robust connections with industries and corporate entities, fostering a dynamic partnership to enhance the quality and relevance of education. Our Board of Studies, composed of influential product manufacturers, plays a pivotal role in advising and collaborating on course development and updates. Their expertise extends beyond the academic realm, actively contributing to post-course training and recruitment initiatives.

The Board of Studies serves as a guiding force, providing valuable assistance in key areas:

1. Syllabus Formulation:

Collaborating closely with industry experts allows us to receive pertinent advice on syllabus development. This ensures that our courses remain aligned with the evolving needs and technological advancements within the respective sectors.

2. Advanced Technology Workshops for Faculty:

To keep our faculty abreast of the latest industry trends and technological advancements, the Board of Studies facilitates Advanced Technology Workshops. These workshops empower our educators with the knowledge and skills necessary to deliver cutting-edge education.

3. In-Course Training/Workshops for Senior Students

For senior students, the Board organizes in-course training and workshops, providing hands-on experiences and practical insights. This proactive approach aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application.

4. On-Job Training in Relevant Industries:

Recognizing the importance of experiential learning, the Board arranges on-the-job training opportunities for our students. This exposure to relevant industries allows them to apply classroom knowledge to real-world scenarios, enhancing their skills and employability.

5. Employment Opportunities:

A crucial aspect of the Board's function is to facilitate employment opportunities for our students. By leveraging their industry connections, the Board ensures that our graduates seamlessly transition into the workforce, securing positions that align with their field of study.

At The George Telegraph Training, our commitment to excellence extends beyond the classroom. The symbiotic relationship with our Board of Studies enriches our educational ecosystem, ensuring that our programs remain dynamic and industry-relevant, and pave the way for successful career trajectories for our students.
