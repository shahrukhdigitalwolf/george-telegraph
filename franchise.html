Heritage Scheme for Beauty & Wellness

The George Telegraph Group's latest endeavor is George Telegraph College of Beauty & Wellness. This Institute deals with one of the fastest growing sectors of the Indian economy. Heritage Scheme for Paramedical Science

There is a steady demand for Indian paramedical personnel around the world. This demand is not only for their knowledge and skills. Heritage Scheme for Full ATC

The George Telegraph Training Institute started franchisee operations in the year 1997. Since then this Institute went ahead to open franchisee training centres i.e. Authorized Training Centres (ATCs) in almost every corner of eastern India covering West Bengal, Odisha, Jharkhand, Bihar and Assam.

In an ATC numerous disciplines like Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Computer Hardware, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Software and Commercial courses are taught under the same roof.

The advantages of an ATC are:

  • Due to presence of a number of disciplines, the ATC can target to get a lot many students.
  • Profitability is very high in this case, thereby ensuring a steady rate of returns.
  • An ATC can be opened almost in any geographical location as the wide range of courses taught in an ATC has courses that support all sorts of geographical, economical, social backgrounds.
  • The huge product portfolio ensures low risks.
