Lab technician course and training is an option if you want to work in the medical field without having to go through the rigors of an MBBS. The training produces professionals who are equipped with the knowledge needed for disease diagnosis, treatment, and prevention. Lab assistants can help with tasks connected to the lab and operate and administer medical tests and equipment. The primary topics of the medical lab technician are biochemistry, pathology, microbiology, and blood banking.

Laboratory Technician courses are a 360-degree learning program. The medical field is pretty dynamic in nature. Students can encapsulate a number of opportunities based on their skill set. Based on their set of interests and advanced skills; they can choose the path they feel fit for their career ahead

  • Pursue a career as a Microbiology Laboratory Technician
  • Learn how to diagnose diseases
  • Receive guidelines on how to get a job in this field

Students are eligible to join The The George Telegraph Training Institute’s Lab technician DMLT course after 12th. After the lab technician training program, you will be fit to pursue the above-mentioned fields. Additionally, you can get guidelines on how you can switch your area of specialization too.
