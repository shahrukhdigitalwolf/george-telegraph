Department Of Beauty & Wellness

George Telegraph has been shaping careers using over well-defined 80 vocational courses, especially beauty parlor courses for aspirants across different strata of society - from the grassroots to the well- heeled.

Application Form

We stand out from the clutter with our Industry - compatible curricula, and meaningful teaching and training at affordable rates. We are an ISO 9001:2015 certified registered trust body. George Telegraph Training Institute (GTTI) has come a long way since its inception 101 years ago, spearheading the quest for excellence of the nation's youth by way of providing cutting-edge teaching and training through exclusive and in sync vocational courses. We are offering the most top-notch professional beautician training course in Kolkata.

The growth is evidenced in the fact that, from one unit in Sealdah, Kolkata in 1920, GTTI today has more than 70 training centers, beautician training institute in Kolkata and 25,000 students across the eastern states of India.

Our courses across our demographic presence are recognized by the National Skills Development Corporation (N.S.D.C.) which is under The Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Govt of India. Many of our courses are also affiliated to Netaji Subhas Open University (N.S.O.U) which is under UGC.

The Beauty and Wellness Industry in India is growing every year at a pace that is twice the growth rate of the beauty & wellness industry in the U.S.A. and the European markets.

With the introduction of smartphones and internet in every nook and corner of India, the consumers in India are getting awareness about the products that the beauty & wellness industry can offer. That is creating a greater demand for products from this industry. That has created a shortage in trained manpower.
