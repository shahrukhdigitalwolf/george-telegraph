Accounts And Taxation Department

The George Telegraph Training Institute commenced operations in 1920 and since then it has been dedicated to the empowerment of youth through training in vocational trades. As a pioneer in the field, the Institute quickly gained a formidable reputation and its excellence in training was recognized by diverse authorities like the British Army, the Indian Defence Forces, Maritime Authorities and the British Railway Board.

What is Finance & Accounting?

Through courses such as the Certificate in Financial Accounting and professional courses in finance and accounting, individuals can gain comprehensive knowledge of financial analysis, budgeting, investment planning, risk management, and financial decision-making. The George Telegraph Training Institute offers comprehensive accounting certification courses and accounting and finance certification courses to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge for a successful accounting career.

What is Taxation?

Taxation is a crucial aspect of financial management that deals with the process of imposing and collecting taxes from individuals and businesses by the government. For aspiring accounts and tax students, understanding taxation is essential as it involves knowledge of tax laws, regulations, and compliance. The George Telegraph Training Institute offers comprehensive courses that equip students with the necessary skills to navigate the complex world of taxation. Through these courses, students learn about different types of taxes, tax planning, tax returns, and tax implications for individuals and businesses. By gaining expertise in taxation, aspiring accounts and tax students can contribute effectively to financial decision-making and compliance, making them valuable assets in the accounting and finance industry.
