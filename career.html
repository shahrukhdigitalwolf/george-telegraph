Careers @ GTTI

Working with The George Telegraph Training Institute is a rewarding blend of innovative thinking, collaboration of expertise and multi-functional knowledge sharing. Our unwavering focus is enhancing skill and potential for employment for the new generation with the objective of attaining long-term success. And thus, we are driven by challenges only to convert them into opportunities, making a positive difference by leveraging all available opportunities at the same time.

Here every employee is given the opportunity to assume roles of responsibility. We have an inspiring leadership that encourages transparency of approach across all levels. We are geared by an atmosphere of learning and opportunities for professional as well as personal development.

So if you are passionate about education, you may find the right opportunity here at GTTI. We also have several other requirements in Administrative Positions and other functional roles. So please send us your resume online or get in touch with the office.
